Below are some of the projects I have done in my courses throughout my time at OSU so far.

Revenge Tour Cancelled

Revenge Tour Cancelled is a game I created with a partner in autumn of 2018. Developed for our Fundamentals of Engineering for Honors class, Revenge Tour Cancelled was created for the FEH Proteus custom microcontroller. I programmed the game in C++ using QT Creator and the custom FEH Proteus libraries. The design project was completed in under a week, including documentation, working code, and promotional material. FEH Robot Project (#BuckeyeBots)

This is my currently ongoing school robot project. Working in a team of four, our job is to complete the course set up by the FEH staff. Provided with a budget of $160 and around two months time to move from planning to competition, our team must build an autonomous robot and document every step along the way. In this project I also worked with the FEH Proteus microcontroller, C++, and QT Creator. However, I've picked some new skills up along the way. Using SolidWorks CAD, I designed the main clamp arm to be implemented into our robot. Furthermore, I worked with my team in the workshop to cut parts, solder, and wire components. Furthermore, this project has taught great methods for teamwork and project management
